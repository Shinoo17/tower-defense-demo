# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Players who want a friendly, immediately readable browser tower-defense game on desktop, tablet, or phone. They need to understand the battlefield quickly, place and improve towers with minimal input friction, and move between campaign, replay, and endless sessions without losing context.

## Product Purpose

Skeleton Siege is a compact 3D tower-defense game built around the Kenney Tower Defense Kit and KayKit skeletons. Success means that its five-level campaign, three difficulty modes, replay flow, and Endless Mode remain dependable while the scene, controls, and interface feel equally playable with a mouse or touch screen.

## Brand Personality

Elegant, friendly, and adventurous. The game should feel like a polished fantasy diorama: inviting rather than grim, tactile rather than futuristic, and confident without visual noise.

## Anti-references

Generic admin dashboards, sci-fi HUDs, neon or purple glow-heavy game menus, excessive glass panels, cluttered mobile overlays, emoji-led interfaces, and dark fantasy treatments that obscure the cheerful low-poly assets.

## Design Principles

- Keep the battlefield as the main character and let interface elements frame it.
- Make the next useful action obvious, especially tower placement on touch screens.
- Preserve familiar tower-defense interaction patterns across every viewport.
- Spend motion and detail on orientation, feedback, and atmosphere rather than decoration.
- Scale visual cost to device capability without changing gameplay rules.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and clear keyboard focus. Provide at least 44 by 44 pixel touch targets, safe-area-aware layouts, non-color state cues, readable responsive typography, and complete reduced-motion behavior. Prevent browser gestures from interfering with active play while keeping menus semantically operable.
